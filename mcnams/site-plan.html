Home page

About McnamsAuto

[ McNamsAuto is a subsidiary of the global economic entity McNamsGroupOfCompanies. Mcnams Auto also functions as as single economic entity but with Mcnams GC as the parent company and source of control. Mcnams GC has controlling or majority interest in providing multiple services through different companies. However, McnamsAuto is more dedicated to providing auto machanical services. Repairing automobiles and providing roadside assistance or roadside recovery as part of some other basic level of breakdowns covers.

What users can do on the site

The purpose of the site therefore is to provide users with information on the provided services. Users can visit the site to simply enquire on the machanical services of McNamsAuto or request a machanic both for imidiate response or booking. To more advanced activities like upgrading their breakdown covers or make payments.

24hour standby, callout assistance

The main page of the site provides quick access to a mechanic on standby at any given time of the day. This way, users can get road assistance regardless of whether they have a breakdown during the day or night. This feature makes it easier for customers to also be comfortable travelling any time of the day. Also, for drivers in transit to get imidiate help in case of transporting perishable food that spoil quickly if they stay longer on the road.

Who can use the site

Anyone, from any device can access the information of the site and also make enqureies. However, to complete tasks like payments, the user would need to provide with identification and other necessary documentation. In case of a breakdown, the customer would speak to an agent and provide their location and credibility before a mechanic can make way to the scene. Users will most probably use this site because it is authentic and it also comes as one of a few of it's kind throughout the whole province. Mcnams Auto is one of a few companies which offers road assistance, not just providing roadside recovery but also repairing on the go. To narrow it down, it is the only company which offers these service for 130km radius from it's location.346 Despite having no strong competitors, McnamsAuto still strives to provide the best services for our customers. Hence the effort to create a authentic site for them to utilize in case of emegencies or simply to save them a costly visit to our facilities for simple tasks as booking and appointment with a mechanic or auto servicing.

Regions where McnamsAuto can provide roadside assistance

As mentioned and made aware throughout the site, Mcnams Auto can only provide road assistance to certain regions of the Mpumalanga province. With the headquaters currently located in Belfast, the company can only respond to callouts in the radius of 130 km from it's headquaters but with the exception of high level breakdown covers. The radius ranges from Belfast itself, as far as Nelspruit, Middleburg, Pretoria, Carolina, Dullstroom and many more include in the above mentioned radius.

Types of services

The site will also take advantage and provide the user with the type of services offered by Mcnams Auto. Not just providing roadside assistance, but also buying and selling cars and car parts as well. As an informative page, this site will also provide with access to other sub pages which will provide more and specific information to the users liking. ]
